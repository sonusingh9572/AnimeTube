Privacy Policy for MotionTube

We believe your privacy is important. This simple policy explains how we handle your data:

1. What Information Do We Collect?

  • **Personal Data:** We **do not collect or store** any personal information (such as your name, email, location, or phone number). You can use MotionTube anonymously.
  • **Local Data (On Device):** We store minimal information **locally on your device** only for app function. This includes:
    • Your **video play position** (last seen time) to allow you to resume playback.
    • Your **app settings** and **button states**.
    **This local data is never uploaded to our servers.**

2. Third-Party Content and Network Access

  • **Video Content:** MotionTube fetches video content and metadata (titles, thumbnails, stream links) from **public video platforms** such as **YouTube** and **Dailymotion**.
  • **Network Access:** The app requires internet access to stream content. When you stream videos, the third-party platforms may observe standard network information, such as your **IP Address** and **User-Agent**.
  • We are not responsible for the content or privacy policies of these external platforms.

3. Security

Since we do not handle or transmit sensitive user data, your information remains safe on your device.
